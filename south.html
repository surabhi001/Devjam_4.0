Welcome to South India

Find here the best of authhontic dishes from Andhra Pradesh,Kerala,Telangana and Karnataka which is popular not only in South Indian but throughout the country for it flavours and savory taste.

Gongura Mutton

It is a very popular Andhra mutton curry.Sorrel leaves mutton curry is a spicy curry with distinctive taste of gongura or sorrel leaves.

Ulava Charu

It is a South Indian soup dish from Andhara Pradesh.It is made with Horse gram,a legume with the botanical name Macrotyloma uniflorum,called Ulava in the native Telugu.

Pootha Rekulu

The name of the sweet literally means 'coated sheet'.It stuffed with jaggery and dry fruits.Alternative name of Pootha rekulu is Paper sweet.

Malabar Biryani

Malabar Chicken Biryani is a non vegetarian rice dish that has taken over the food word.This delicacy from the Malabar region of Kerala is very popular,especially amongst Biryani lovers.

Appam and Stew

Popular vegetables based curry from kerala which is also known as ishtu.It is a combination of vegetable creamy curry prepared with thick and thin coconut milk and dry spices.

Payasam

A chilled South Asian dessert made from slow cooked rice,milk and sugar.It is typically flavoured with saffron,various nuts,cashews and almonds.

Sarva Pindi

A traditional dish from telangana made as evening snack crispy.Sarva pindi is a circular shaped pancake made from peanuts.

Qubani Ka Meetha

It is a Hydrabadi sweet made from dried apricots and cream or custard.It is a very popular and traditional dessert from Hydrabad.

Bisi Bele Bath

Bisi Bele Bath is an aromatic and spicy dish originating from the Indian state of Karnataka.

Obbatu

Obbatu is a traditional flatbread which enjoys popularity in Karnataka,obbatu are sweet flat breads made with chana dal stuffing.
