Welcome to East India

East Indian cuisine has a distinct character that sets it apart from the cuisines of other parts of India. Find here the best of regional cuisine from Bihar, West Bengal, North-Eastern States and Odisha that gives us a whole lot of mouth-watering delicacies which are simply irresistible

Litti Chokha

A special delicacy from Bihar, litti chokha is the perfect dish to represent the earthiness of state.

Khaja

Khaja is a popular sweet in Bihar which is essentially the layered fritters dunked in sugar syrup which adds flavours to its crispness.

Macher Jhol

A sumptuous fish curry from the state of West Bengal which is known for its nutritional value and mouth-watering flavours.

Kosha Mangsho

Kosha mangsho is a very popular traditional dish in Bengal which is a delicious spicy Bengali style mutton curry that is full of flavors.

Sandesh

A Bengali meal is simply incomplete without Sandesh. It's an ideal treat because of its creamy soft texture and sweetness.

Lobongo Latika

Lobongo Latika is a rich and aromatic desert made of nuts and mawa which perfectly exemplifies the opulent side of Indian cuisine.

Thukpa

Another comfort food from the North East that we can never get enough of, thukpa is a soupy dish which is extremely satiating and nutritious.

Masor Tenga

A delectable fish curry from the beautiful state of Assam in which Rohu fish marinated in a tangy and paste of spices and cooked in luscious gravy.

Dalma

A traditional recipe from Odisha, dalma is nutritionally rich with split chickpeas cooked along with raw papaya, potatoes and a host of masalas.

Khar

Khar is one of the very popular and unique dish of Assam. This is traditional dish made of young raw papaya which gives the already aromatic papaya a sweet aroma.
