Welcome to North India

Find here the best of authentic dishes from Punjab, Uttar Pradesh, Madhya pradesh, Uttrakhand, Delhi and Haryana which is popular not only in North Indian but known throughout the country for it flavours and savory taste.

Indian Fish Amritsari (Fried White Fish)

This fried fish dish is named after the city where it originated (Amritsar). Use firm-fleshed white fish to make this great appetizer or side dish to complement the main course.

Indian Chickpea Curry (Chole)

Chickpea curry or chole is a crowd-pleaser when served piping hot and accompanied by pooris/bhatooras and mostly used as main course in Indian functions.

Jalebi deep fried dessert

This crispy, syrupy dessert is well-loved everywhere in India. It can be compared to that perennial favorite fair food served in the States—funnel cakes—except at the end, these fried treats are soaked in a sugary syrup.

Indian Rice Pudding (Kheer)

This creamy rice pudding is delicately flavored with cardamom and is full of nuts. It's a great dessert for any time of the year. In South India, where it is known as payasam, and East India, where it is called payesh, certain versions are made for special festivals.

Indian Layered Bread (Lacha Paratha)

Crispy and multi-layered, this Indian flatbread is a great accompaniment for any thick-gravied, hearty Indian dish, especially tikka masala or masala gosht, for example.

Indian Butter Chicken (Murg Makhani)

This dish is among the best-known Indian foods all over the world even though it is relatively new to the cuisine. It was created at the Moti Mahal hotel in Delhi in the 1950s. Its creamy gravy can be made as hot or as mild as you like.

Mutter paneer peas cottage cheese (Mutter Paneer)

Delicately flavored paneer (farmer's cheese or dry curd cheese) is teamed with peas, in a tangy gravy. ​Mutter paneer ​is probably the most frequently ordered vegetarian dish in Indian restaurants.

Indian Spinach and Cottage Cheese (Palak Paneer)

This mildly flavored dish is super healthy too! If paneer (farmer's cheese or dry curd cheese) is not on hand or you want a dairy-free option, you also can use tofu or veggies like cauliflower or potato.

Indian Red Kidney Bean Curry
(Rajma Dal)

This Indian gravy is highly appriated for its mouth watering taste and nutritions. Serve this red kidney bean curry or rajma dal with plain boiled rice, kachumber salad, and your favorite pickle.
